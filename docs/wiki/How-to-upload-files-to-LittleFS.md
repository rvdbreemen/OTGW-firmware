- Install the LittleFS upload plugin for your Arduino IDE

https://github.com/earlephilhower/arduino-esp8266littlefs-plugin

- Upload the **data** directory to the LittleFS

Before you upload data to LittleFS make sure to put your NodeMCU into flashmode (do this by: hold flash and reset, then release flash)
When you have done these steps, just reboot your NodeMCU. And plug it back into your OTGW, and hook it up.
